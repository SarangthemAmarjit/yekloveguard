Privacy Policy

Yek Love Checker

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Yek Love Checker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. App Purpose

Yek Love Checker is a cultural compatibility app designed for the Meitei community of Manipur. It helps users verify Yek Salai lineage compatibility based on surnames, following traditional customs.

The app is intended for informational and cultural awareness purposes only.

2. Information You Enter

The app allows users to enter surnames to check Yek compatibility.

The entered surnames are processed locally within the app.

The app uses a Yek Salai dataset stored inside the app (JSON file in assets).

We do not store, collect, transmit, or save user-entered data.

No personal data is sent to any server.

All compatibility checking happens entirely offline within the app.

3. Local Data Usage

The Yek Salai lineage data used in the app is stored locally within the application package.
The app does not download or update this data from external servers.

4. Advertising

Yek Love Checker uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

Device information

Advertising ID

App interaction data

General location (country-level)

This information is collected by Google to provide relevant ads and support free app usage.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal user information.

Any data collected for advertising purposes is handled by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob according to their own privacy policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

Yek Love Checker is intended for general audiences.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Cultural Disclaimer

This app provides compatibility information based on traditional Yek Salai lineage understanding. It is not a legal, religious, or official authority decision. Users are encouraged to consult elders or community authorities for final decisions.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with a revised effective date.
